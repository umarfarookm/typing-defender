How to Play

Objective

Type the falling words before they reach the bottom of the screen. Survive as long as possible and achieve the highest score!

Controls

  • Type letters on your keyboard to match falling words
  • Press ESC to pause/resume
  • Matching characters highlight in green

Scoring

  • 3-5 letter words: 5 points
  • 6-8 letter words: 10 points
  • 9-12 letter words: 20 points
  • 13+ letter words: 30 points

Combos

Every 5 consecutive words without a miss increases your combo multiplier (x2, x3, x4...).

Power-Ups

  • ❄️ Freeze Time — Stops all words for 5 seconds
  • ⭐ Double Score — Double points for 10 seconds
  • 💣 Bomb — Destroys every active word
  • 💚 Extra Life — Adds one life
  • 🐌 Slow Motion — Reduces falling speed

Difficulty

Every 30 seconds the level increases. Words fall faster and more appear simultaneously.
